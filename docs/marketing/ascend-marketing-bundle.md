**Technical notes for editor:**
- Capture all 6 in-app shots with iOS built-in screen recording at maximum resolution.
- Hide the system status bar via dev menu before recording (clean top edge).
- Overlay text: SF Pro Display Bold, 72pt, white with 2px black stroke for legibility on light backgrounds in SHOT 1–3.
- No music sting on shot transitions — let the haptic/UI pulse from the app carry rhythm.
